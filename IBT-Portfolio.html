Career Planning





Chartered (Professional) Accountant

A career that seems promising to me in the future is a CA or CPA which stands for Chartered Accountant or Chartered Professional Accountant. The reason why a job in the accounting field comes to my interest is due to the fact that I am simply in love with numbers. They’ve always dazzled me because everywhere you look has a connection to numbers whether it be dimensions or an object or how it was made with the correct quantities. Along with this career option, you must have a background of business and I do enjoy business. Now in order to become a CA or CPA, I can take a number of programs in university, however, I’m more interested in what Laurier has to offer me with their program. I have to obtain a BBA degree which can be possible with the following courses in high school:

  • English – ENG4UO
  • Calculus and Vectors – MCV4UO
  • Data Management - MDM4UO
  • Advance Functions – MHF4UO
